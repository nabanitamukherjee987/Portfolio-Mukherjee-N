About Me

Hey there! I’m someone who’s always been fascinated by how life works—from cells and DNA to entire ecosystems. That curiosity led me to study biology, but along the way, I picked up a love for tech and data too. Now, I blend both worlds by using computer informatics to dig into biological questions, whether that’s through coding, analyzing data, or building tools that help scientists make sense of complex systems. I enjoy solving problems, learning new things, and finding cool ways to connect biology with technology. If it involves biology, data, and a good challenge, I’m in!
